ABOUT ME

I am a UI/UX designer with experience in marketing and product development. Digital is a new default that’s why it’s impossible to imagine any business without website or app. An excellent product, idea or service is only a part of success. It may fail for many reasons on its way to the user. My goal is to help companies to increase their profits by creating comfortable and seamless digital interaction with a product for customers. My role is to create website and application that are convenient and easy to use for people based not on assumption about what we think user needs but on deep professional research and analysis. I imagine myself as a bridge for the companies on their way to success. Biggest satisfaction will be to see the result of work – high income for the companies, happy and satisfied customers. Just boosted my skills finishing Georgia Tech boot camp in UI/UX design, and can’t wait to make digital world a better place for people!
